About the Conference

SolarWinds Innovate is headed back to Brno, Czech Republic on December 14th, 2017!

Innovate Summit is a one-day event and a great opportunity for you to meet and learn from SolarWinds engineers and others in the monitoring, management and development community. Innovate Summit 2016 was a blast!

Spend the evening hearing talks from experts and fellow devs on topics within the cloud native ecosystem, ranging from containers to serverless. Finish the day off with a social happy hour, where you can network and just get to know your local technology community a little better.

We are passionate about running infrastructure and applications at-scale, and we're excited to pull together a group that shares that passion and is interested in sharing how they build and maintain complex distributed systems.
