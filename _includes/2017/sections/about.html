About The Conference

SolarWinds Innovate is headed to Brno, Czech Republic on December 14th, 2017!

Innovate is a one-day event and a great opportunity for you to meet and learn from SolarWinds engineers and others in the monitoring community.

About Brno

The students’ city. Czech’s Silicon Valley. Second largest. A capital of Moravia. A lot of epithets describe this vibrant city full of cafés, bars, bistros, high-tech companies, and three large universities with more than 90,000 students in total. Each year, Brno hosts plenty of cultural and sporting events, expos, and technical conferences.
