About The Conference

SolarWinds Innovate is headed to Brno, Czech Republic on December 14th, 2017!

Innovate is a one-day event and a great opportunity for you to meet and learn from SolarWinds engineers and others in the monitoring community.

Spend the day hearing talks from experts and fellow devs on topics ranging from in-field issues to cutting-edge technology that you should be keeping an eye on. Finish the day off with a social happy hour, where you can network and just get to know your local monitoring community a little better.

We are passionate about monitoring solutions, and we're excited to pull together a group that shares that passion and is interested in keeping up with how the industry is growing and evolving.
